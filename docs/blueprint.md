# **App Name**: Dhanwanthri Healing

## Core Features:

- Comprehensive Service Overview: Present detailed information on all clinic services, including Ayurvedic therapies, Advanced Sports Therapy (Dry Needling, Kinesiology, Manipulation), HBOT, and Hydrocolon therapy, clearly outlining their benefits and approach.
- Multimedia Clinic Showcase: Display high-quality photo galleries and video containers illustrating clinic facilities, including the upgraded 12-room facility and specialized rooms (HBOT, Hydrocolon), as well as demonstrations of various treatment procedures and the interior of the clinic.
- Physician Profile: Feature a dedicated section for Dr. Dharmesh Kubendiran's qualifications, extensive experience in Ayurvedic medicine, Sports kinesiology, sports science, and biomechanics, highlighting his expertise.
- Interactive Content Search & Q&A: An AI-powered tool for users to ask questions about specific treatments, conditions, or the clinic's methodology, and receive intelligent, contextually relevant answers drawn directly from the provided extensive website content.
- Online Appointment & Contact Forms: Provide a user-friendly form for prospective patients to easily submit detailed inquiries or request appointment bookings, improving accessibility and reducing administrative overhead.
- Patient Success Stories: Display a collection of patient testimonials and success stories to build trust and demonstrate the effectiveness and impact of the clinic's treatments on various conditions.
- Dynamic Content Management: Enable clinic staff to easily upload, organize, and manage all textual content, images, and videos across the website without requiring developer intervention, ensuring up-to-date information on services and facilities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated and calming muted blue-green (#3D707A) for a professional and trustworthy feel, blending elements of healing and precision in medicine.
- Background color: A very light, clean blue-green (#ECF2F4) to create an airy, tranquil, and clear canvas that emphasizes content legibility within a light scheme.
- Accent color: A vibrant yet balanced turquoise-green (#26DBB5) to draw attention to interactive elements, calls-to-action, and key highlights without overpowering the primary palette, while maintaining an energetic and fresh vibe.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, techy, and scientific feel, suitable for showcasing advanced therapies and scientific expertise. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its neutral, objective, and highly legible characteristics, ensuring clarity in detailed medical explanations.
- Use clear, precise, and professional line icons. Icons should visually represent medical treatments, wellness, sports, and traditional Ayurvedic symbols in a modern, minimalistic style.
- Adopt a clean, structured, and highly responsive layout, ensuring optimal readability and navigation on all devices. Prioritize clear visual hierarchy to guide users through extensive content, utilizing prominent photo and video containers for engaging visuals of the clinic and treatments.
- Implement subtle and smooth animations for page transitions, content loading, and interactive elements such as forms and buttons. Animations should enhance user experience without being distracting, maintaining a professional and fluid feel.
